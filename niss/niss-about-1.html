Our Story

From a Small Town
to a Global Mindset

NISS was founded in 2010 in Malegaon, Maharashtra, with a simple but ambitious belief — that world-class software doesn't need to come from a big city. It needs to come from talented, dedicated people.

What started as a small web development studio has grown into a full-service IT company, delivering custom software, ERPs, mobile apps, and e-commerce platforms to clients across India and beyond.

2010 — Foundation
NISS founded in Malegaon. First clients: local businesses needing websites and basic software automation.
2013 — ERP Growth
Expanded into educational ERP solutions. Launched Wigital Campus, now used by multiple institutions.
2017 — Mobile & E-Commerce
Built the Ummid Android app and launched full e-commerce solutions. Client base doubled.
2024 — Present
30+ active clients, 50+ projects delivered. Full-service digital agency with cloud, branding, and SEO capabilities.
