Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a freelance web developer in Mumbai charge?
Professional website development in Mumbai starts at Rs 2,499 per month with a one-time setup fee of Rs 3,999. All plans include hosting, SSL, and maintenance. There are no hidden charges or surprise bills at the end of the month.
Do you provide SEO services along with web development?
Yes. The Business Plan and above include full on-page SEO, technical SEO, Google Business Profile setup, SEO blog writing, and monthly keyword reports. The Growth Plan adds local SEO for Mumbai and across India, backlinks, and competitor research.
How long does it take to build and launch a website?
Most websites are live within 7 to 14 days after requirements are confirmed and initial content is provided. The Starter Plan can be completed in under one week for businesses that need a fast launch.
Is hosting included in all plans?
Yes. Every plan includes hosting, SSL certificate, and ongoing maintenance. You do not need to purchase any separate hosting or manage any server configuration. Everything is handled for you.
Can I upgrade from Starter to a higher plan later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time. You only pay the difference in setup fee, prorated for the current month. There is no penalty and the upgrade process takes less than 48 hours.
Do you work with businesses outside Mumbai?
Yes. While based in Mumbai, I work with businesses from all across India including Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, and more. International clients in USD are also welcome. All communication is done remotely via WhatsApp, email, or video call.
