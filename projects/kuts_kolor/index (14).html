Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability, especially for popular services like hair coloring, treatments, and bridal packages. You can book by calling us or using our online booking system.

We use only premium, salon-grade products from renowned brands. Our hair coloring services use ammonia-free options when possible, and our keratin treatments are formaldehyde-free for your safety and best results.

A keratin treatment typically takes 2-4 hours depending on your hair length and thickness. The results last 3-6 months with proper care. We recommend a consultation before the treatment to assess your hair condition.

Yes! We offer comprehensive bridal packages that include pre-bridal treatments, trial sessions, and day-of bridal makeup and hairstyling. Our bridal specialist will work with you to create your perfect wedding look. Contact us for customized packages.

We accept cash, all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and bank transfers. For bridal packages, we also offer flexible payment plans with advance booking.

Yes, there is street parking available on Lamington Road and nearby areas. The salon is also easily accessible by public transport - Mumbai Central station is just a short walk away.
