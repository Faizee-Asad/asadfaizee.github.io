Our Story

A Celebration of India's Culinary Heritage

Native Bombay pays homage to India's diverse culinary heritage, celebrating the flavors we often overlook. By embracing indigenous ingredients and traditional recipes, we serve dishes that resonate with memories of home while defying the norms of typical Indian fine-dining.

Nestled inside IF:BE — a restored 150-year-old ice factory in Ballard Estate, Fort — the architecture itself is the experience. Exposed brick, original timber, and the industrial bones of history frame every memorable meal.
