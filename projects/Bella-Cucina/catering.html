Questions

Frequently Asked

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for smaller events and 3–6 months for large events like weddings. However, we'll always do our best to accommodate shorter timelines — reach out and we'll let you know our availability.
We primarily serve Chicago and the greater Chicagoland area including the suburbs. For large events (100+ guests) we may be able to travel further — please mention your location in your inquiry and we'll confirm availability.
Absolutely. We can accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free, halal, kosher-style, and most other dietary needs. We'll create customized menu options for any dietary requirement — just let us know during the consultation.
Yes, we require a 25% non-refundable deposit to hold your event date, with the balance due 7 days before your event. For events over $10,000, a custom payment schedule can be arranged. We accept all major credit cards and bank transfer.
The Completo and Gran Festa packages include full china, glassware, silverware, and linen napkins. The Essenziale package uses premium biodegradable service ware. Additional rental items (tablecloths, floral, décor) can be arranged through our preferred vendors.
For events of 50 or more guests, we offer a complimentary tasting session at the restaurant for up to 2 people. You'll sample a curated selection of dishes from your proposed menu. For smaller events, a tasting can be arranged for a small fee that is credited toward your booking.
