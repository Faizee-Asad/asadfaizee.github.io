Our Story

Nonna's Recipes,
Chicago's Heart

Bella Cucina was born from a simple dream — to bring the soulful, sun-drenched flavors of Southern Italy to the streets of downtown Chicago. Founded in 2009 by the Esposito family, our kitchen has always been guided by the recipes passed down from our grandmothers in Calabria.

Every dish is a love letter to the Italian south — bold, unpretentious, and crafted with ingredients sourced from local farms and trusted Italian producers we've worked with for over a decade.
