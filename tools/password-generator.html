FAQ

How secure are these passwords?

All passwords are generated client‑side using crypto.getRandomValues() – a cryptographically secure random number generator. No data is sent to our server.

What password length should I use?

For most services, 12–16 characters with a mix of character types is sufficient. For highly sensitive accounts, consider 20+ characters.

Can I generate multiple passwords?

Yes, you can generate up to 10,000 passwords at once. Useful for bulk account creation or testing.

What are ambiguous characters?

Characters that look similar, like il1Lo0O, which can be easily confused when reading or typing. Excluding them improves readability.
