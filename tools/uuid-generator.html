FAQ

What is UUID?

UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) is a 128‑bit identifier used to uniquely identify records, users, sessions, files and many other resources.

Is this UUID Generator secure?

Yes. UUIDs are generated directly in your browser. No data is sent to our server.

Can I generate multiple UUIDs?

Yes. You can generate up to 10,000 UUIDs at once.

Which UUID version should I use?

UUID v4 (random) is recommended for most applications. UUID v7 is time‑sortable. UUID v1 includes a timestamp and MAC address.
