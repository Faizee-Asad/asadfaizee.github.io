Why SS-26

A sandal that thinks
in four parts.

Most sandals are one molded piece fighting your foot. SS-26 separates concerns: cage holds, footbed cushions, tread grips. Each part is replaceable, recyclable, and tuned for the way you actually walk.

01 · Cage

Breathable one-piece upper

3D-printed polymer shell with 1,200 micro-perforations. Wet-dry, hot-cold, it breathes with your foot instead of trapping it.

02 · Footbed

Anatomical cork-blend

A contoured cork + EVA footbed that molds to your arch over the first ten miles, then holds. Removable, washable, replaceable.

03 · Outsole

Grip-grade vulcanized rubber

3.5mm lug pattern tuned for wet rock, packed dirt and city concrete. Siped for water channeling, not just looks.

04 · Bungee

Quick-lace closure system

One-pull bungee with a glove-friendly toggle. Adjust tension on the fly — tighter for trail, looser for the train.

05 · Stack

Replaceable, not disposable

Every part is a serviceable module. Worn out the outsole after 800 miles? Swap it. Keep the rest. Less waste, longer life.

06 · Verified

Field-tested for 18 months

Two years of prototype testing across the Pacific Northwest, the Alps and Tokyo — 4,200 miles logged by 38 testers.
